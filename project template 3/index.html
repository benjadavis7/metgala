Sleeping Beauty: Reawakening Fashion

At its core, "Sleeping Beauties" is a celebration of fashion that withstands the passage of time. The theme draws inspiration from classic fairy tales like Sleeping Beauty, emphasizing the idea of garments, textiles, and styles that lie dormant, only to be revived and appreciated in a new light. It invites designers and attendees to explore how fashion can be both a relic of the past and a living, breathing art form that evolves and transforms with each reawakening.

"Sleeping Beauties" is more than just a whimsical nod to fairy tales; it serves as a metaphor for the fashion worldâ€™s ongoing commitment to preservation, sustainability, and the celebration of timeless beauty. By highlighting the art of fashion conservation, the theme emphasizes the value of preserving the past while breathing new life into it, much like how Sleeping Beauty herself was awakened from her slumber.

The theme also resonates with current discussions around sustainable fashion and the importance of valuing and reusing what already exists. In a world where the fashion industry is increasingly focusing on sustainability, "Sleeping Beauties" invites a reexamination of how old garments can inspire new creations, reducing waste and celebrating craftsmanship.

Our Mission

The Met Gala is dedicated to celebrating the intersection of fashion, art, and culture by bringing together a diverse and dynamic community of creative minds. As an annual fundraising event for The Metropolitan Museum of Art's Costume Institute, the Gala aims to inspire, challenge, and elevate the world of fashion while honoring its profound impact on society. Through innovative themes, iconic style, and a commitment to the arts, the Met Gala seeks to spark conversations, foster creativity, and support the preservation and advancement of fashion as a vital form of artistic expression.
